Introduction

Welcome to Unicorn Kids Bilingual School. I would like to use this opportunity to introduce the background of our school via this website.

Unicorn Kids Bilingual School was established in 2018 to nurture the kids by widening their horizon by enhancing their language abilities, through a series of hands-on activities and experiences. Due to the worldwide phenomenon of globalization, there is and will be potential demand of acquiring an additional set of language skills.

In the increasingly globalized world we live in, more than half of the population is bilingual or multilingual. In order to thrive in a diverse society, an additional language not only puts your child ahead of others in terms of job opportunities, but also is an asset which helps develop essential cognitive skills, such as creativity, critical thinking, problem solving, and logic.

Our Ideology and Vision
Our Ideology

Established in 2018 in Tokyo as a school teaching an international curriculum in English and Mandarin. Our school’s mission is to nurture today’s children through extending their horizon by designing an educational environment which is both fun and effective for children’s learning. We believe providing children with hands on experience can be more rewarding than conventional studies.

Our Vision

In the process of learning to converse in English, the school will provide opportunities for children to apply what they have learned in a practical manner. For example, when children come across a new vocabulary, the teacher will make sure the student fully comprehends the word through creating sentences and reusing it in different contexts. In addition, we stress on the importance of children’s moral education. We believe establishing a foundation for ethics and fundamental etiquette for communication is essential in the world of rapid globalization such as understanding the significance of gratitude and becoming future contributors to society. We also help children discover their identity, such as managing their emotions, building self-confidence, learning to respect and share with others, and teamwork building capabilities. Extra-curricular activities are provided and included in the daily schedule, including: music, calligraphy, gymnastics, art and dance. We help children realize their potential and what they are skillful at to further develop their talents. We are strong advocates for learning happily and developing their interests in the process. Classics of different languages are played throughout the day to stimulate the children’s brain development and learn to be able to adjust. Other opportunities will be provided to help children train and develop the abilities to deal with various tasks and appropriately handle different matters to help them grow stronger and be better prepared.

Our school strongly urges the parents to be fully involved in their children’s education.
